About Us

Welcome to Atta Son's (SaimTech), where we believe that running a business should be simple and hassle-free. Our mission is to empower small and medium-sized businesses with innovative, easy-to-use, and free Point of Sale (POS) software that transforms the way you manage your operation.

What sets us apart?

Simplicity:

Our intuitive interface ensures that you and your staff can start using our POS system with minimal training. We prioritize ease of use, so you can focus on what matters most—growing your business.

As a cloud-based solution, our POS software allows you to access your business data anytime, anywhere. Whether you're at the counter, on the go, or working from home, you have complete control at your fingertips.

We believe that every business, regardless of size, deserves access to top-tier tools. That's why our POS software is completely free, with no hidden fees or surprise charges.
